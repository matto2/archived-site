UX Story 5: Process Examples


My process normally consists of a lot of conversations and whiteboarding sessions with Product managers, engineers, and other stakeholders. The goal being to define and understand the problem first before diving in and falling in love with any particular solution. I also try to get additional information from sales, support and/or sales engineers to get a deeper understanding of the problem actual customers are dealing with. From there I do a lot of thinking, research and sketching in my notebook, often while sitting with PM. This process helps create a shared understanding of the direction we're headed to solve the problem prior to any pixels being pushed around.

Top right is a shot of my sketches of an account dropdown and initial screens made while sitting with the Product Manager. As you can see there's a lot of fast editing and revisions. Next are screen captures of the visual design.
