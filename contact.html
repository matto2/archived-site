About

O’LEARY STUDIOS features the visual design and art of Matt O’Leary. Matt co-founded The Juice, a regional surfing magazine, established and ran the digital prepress department at West Marine and has served as designer and print production manager for Envision Media and McDill Associates. At FastPencil he specialized in book cover design, product development and marketing for the online publishing technology platform. Matt is currently focused on a variey of web and book projects.

Matt is a lifelong artist and currently paints with oils and acrylics on board or canvas. Growing up in Santa Cruz, California, he discovered a love of surfing and the beauty of the coast early on and it has been a favorite subject of his art ever since.

For information on art or design work, contact the artist.
