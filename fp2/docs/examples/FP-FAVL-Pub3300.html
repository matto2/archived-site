My Favorite Book

By Amy Reggio

  • First Edition
  • Language(s): ENG
  • 24 pages
  • Friends of African Village Libraries favlafrica
  • Published on December 20th, 2011
Available Formats:
8.5"x 8.5" color paperback

This book depicts children in Burkina Faso holding their favorite books and offering reasons as to why they prefer them over others. Upon completion, the reader may feel inclined to seek out those books. In so doing, this book instills the reader with an enthusiasm for reading by showing that it is enjoyable and interesting while giving them skills that will aid them in the future.
