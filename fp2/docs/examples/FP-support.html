FastPencil Tips

Facebook

Did you know you can login to FastPencil using your Facebook login? It's fast, easy and lets you share your progress with friends!

Speakers

Many professional speakers use FastPencil to publish books to sell at their speaking engagements. Just grab your notes, throw them into FastPencil, fill in some stories and publish. Your audience will love you for it!

Teachers

We have seen many teachers use FastPencil to help their class publish a book of poetry or short stories. It's a fun and easy way to get your students working together, and they can publish and get a copy to save forever!

Expand your experiences

Write about what you know, while you're brewing up your masterpiece. You can have lots of projects going on at the same time on FastPencil. Look around and start a project about something you love, like gardening, or stories your Dad used to tell you...it's a fun way to keep writing, and you can quickly and easily turn these into eBooks.

E-books

It's better to publish 10 short eBooks on topics you know, then to publish one huge eBook on multiple topics. Get your ideas out fast and build up a following of readers and fans.

Revisions

If you happen to do a bunch of writing one night, after a few glasses of wine, and you come back the next day to find your story has taken a turn for the worse... have no fear! Just use the Revisions link and you can roll back to your previous version with one click.
