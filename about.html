O'Leary Studios features selected designs of Matt O’Leary. Matt is an accomplished creative professional focused on User Experience and Interface Design. Currently a UX/UI Designer at Accellion, Matt has a varied background in design: He cofounded The Juice (a regional surfing magazine), established and ran the prepress department at West Marine and has served as designer and production manager for Envision Media and McDill Associates. At FastPencil he specialized in product and user interface design, book cover design, and marketing for the cloud publishing platform. Matt is also a lifelong artist—check out his art at clubofthewaves.com
